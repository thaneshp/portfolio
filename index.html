About Me

Hi, my name is Thanesh, I'm a recent Computing and Software Systems graduate from Melbourne University, passionate about all things development.

I've been into programming and development since high school and haven't stopped since. I am developer because I love problem solving and becoming a better individual everyday. I love to learn about new technology and use this knowledge to create something that has the potential to help others. Personally, I am excited by the thought of being able to constantly learn more in this ever-growing field.

When I'm not busy developing or learning new tech, I enjoy driving to places of nature to get some fresh air. Before covid, my free time often consisted of walking on the beach or exploring the peaceful hills of Melbourne.

If you'd like to learn about what makes me tick or simply have a chat, head over to my contact page.
